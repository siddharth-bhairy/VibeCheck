What is sentiment analysis?

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining, is an automated process that uses natural language processing (NLP) to identify and extract subjective information from text. It categorizes opinions expressed in a piece of text as positive, negative, or neutral.

This technology is a powerful tool for understanding the underlying emotion, attitude, or feeling of the author.

How are comments collected?

To analyze a YouTube video, we use the official YouTube Data API to collect the comments. When you provide a video URL, our system sends a secure request to the API to retrieve all available comments and their associated details.

This process is automated and allows us to gather large volumes of data efficiently and in compliance with YouTube's terms of service. Once the comments are collected, they are passed to our sentiment analysis models for processing.
